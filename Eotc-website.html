Eastern Orthodox Christianity

Eastern Orthodox Christianity is a branch of Christianity that traces its roots to the early Christian Church and is centered in the Eastern Mediterranean region.

Orthodox Christianity is one of the most unique religions out there and stands out to broad Christianity.It has been preserved through several thousand years oF history.

Church History Before the Bible:

This goes deep into the church's history without the Bible, just the Old Testament. Mind you, the church was there before the Bible was created. For example, in St.Paul’s letter to Galatians, it was clearly written in 48-49 AD. But the gospel was made later in 64 AD. The Orthodox Faith believes in the Nicene Creed , which is a clear statment of their faith overall

The Nicene Creed:

God the Almighty, Who created heaven and earth and all things seen and unseen. We believe in one Lord, Jesus Christ, the Only-Begotten Son of God, begotten of the Father before all ages.Light from Light, true God of true God, begotten not created, of one essence with the Father, by Whom all things were made. Who for us men and for our salvation He came down from Heaven; and was incarnate of the Holy Spirit and the Virgin Mary, and became Man. For our sake, He was crucified under Pontius Pilate; He suffered, died, and was buried. And on the third day He rose from the dead, in fulfillment of the Scriptures; He ascended into Heaven and is seated at the right hand of the Father. He is coming again in His glory to judge the living and the dead, and His kingdom shall have no end. And wee believe in the Holy Spirit, the Lord, the LifeGiver, who proceeds from the Father, Who with the Father and the Son is worshiped and glorified, Who has spoken through the Prophets. We believe in one Holy Universal and apostolic Church And we confess one baptism for the remission of sins. We look for the resurrection of the dead, and the life of the world to come. Amen.

The Orthodox Faith also believes in the 5 Pillars of the Faith, which are 5 concepts of their faith.

The 5 Pillars of the Faith:

1. Mystery of the Trinity

The Mystery of the Trinity states that there is one god but in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.They are all one in three.

Father is the begettetr(Heart for Himself)

Son is the begotten (Word for Himself, Father, and Holy Spirit)

Holy Spirit is the one who proceeds (Life for the Father and the Son)
